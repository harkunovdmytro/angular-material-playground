Usual Mat Card

MatCard have some included components

  • mat-card
  • mat-card-content - primary card content (adds padding)
  • mat-card-actions - container for buttons at the bottom of the card (adds padding). Includes input property "align" = 'start' | 'end'
  • mat-card-footer - section anchored to the bottom of the card

  • mat-card-header - section anchored to the top of the card (adds padding)

    • mat-card-header
    • mat-card-title
    • mat-card-title-group
    • mat-card-subtitle

  • mat-card-image - card image. Stretches the image to the container width

    • mat-card-image
    • mat-card-sm-image
    • mat-card-md-image
    • mat-card-lg-image
    • mat-card-xl-image
    • mat-card-avatar
